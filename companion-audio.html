A Methodology for Bitcoin Ordinal Inscription

This is a companion audio supplement to Sovereign Art: A Methodology for Bitcoin Ordinal Inscription by Beth Parin. It is designed to be used alongside the print or ebook edition of the book — not as a replacement for it.

What you will hear is the heart of the book: why sovereignty matters for artists, what this practice looks like from the inside, the economics of sovereign digital art, and what permanence actually means.

This companion audio was generated using an AI voice as part of a sovereign digital art practice. The written words are entirely Beth Parin's own. The AI provides a voice — a way to bring this work to you in a form you can listen to while driving, walking, or simply sitting with your thoughts. The AI did not write these words. It speaks them. The distinction is everything.
