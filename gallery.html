About the Artist

Prettybrid

Prettybrid is an artist working at the intersection of photography, artificial intelligence, and the permanent ledger of Bitcoin. Each work in the Altered States of Reality collection is conceived, directed, and refined by Prettybrid — using AI as a tool of creative expression rather than a replacement for artistic vision.

All works are inscribed as Bitcoin Ordinals — permanently and immutably recorded on the most secure digital ledger in existence.
